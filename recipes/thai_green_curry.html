Thai Green Curry

This recipe came from here: Thai Green Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4-6 tbsp Thai Green Curry Paste
  • 2 Large Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tsp Fresh Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Lemongrass Paste
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup of Chicken Broth (Low Sodium Preferred)
  • 12 oz (400 g) of Coconut Milk
  • 1-3 tsp of Fish Sauce
  • 1-3 tsp of White Sugar
  • 1/8 tsp of Salt
  • 1 Lime
  • 12 oz (350 g) of Sliced Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 2 Japenese Eggplants (small, 1 cm slices)
  • 1.5 cups of Snow Peas
  • 16 Thai Basil Leaves
  • Green or Red Chillies Slices

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a heavy based skillet or pot over medium high heat
  2. Add curry paste, garlic cloves, fresh ginger, and lemongrass and cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  3. Add chicken broth and coconut milk, mix to dissolve paste
  4. Add in sugar, fish sauce, sugar, and salt to the broth after paste has dissolved
  5. Add in Chicken, stir and then lower heat to medium so its bubbling gently. Cook for 7 minutes
  6. Add eggplants, cook for 5 minutes until soft
  7. Taste sauce. Feel free to add in more fish sauce/salt for a saltier flavor or sugar for a sweeter flavor
  8. Add snow peas, cook for 2 minutes until soften, then stir in basil and lime juice. Sauces should be more thin, do not keep simmering or else it will reduce which we don't want
  9. Garnish with chillies and serve
